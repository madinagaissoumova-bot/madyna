# Design Checklist

## First Pass

- Is the primary CTA obvious?
- Is the title more prominent than secondary copy?
- Is the screen scannable in under five seconds?
- Is there a clear focal point above the fold?

## Visual System

- Typography has a clear scale
- Color usage is consistent
- Components share radius, borders, and shadow logic
- Spacing feels rhythmic instead of random

## Polish

- Hover and focus states are visible
- Empty states and helper text feel designed
- Mobile spacing and tap targets remain comfortable
- Decorative effects support the content instead of fighting it
